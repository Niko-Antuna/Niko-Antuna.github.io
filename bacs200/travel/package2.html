Polynesian Cultural Center VIP Pass

With the Super Ambassador Luau Package receive a private guided tour through 6 island villages throughout the day. Then during the evening enjoy the Ali’I Luau Buffet & Dinner Show. For the night portion experience the “Ha: Breath of Life” show with front two row seating.

Dole Plantation Combo Tour

Included in this package is a train tour of the plantation and the history behind it as well as the techniques used in producing the rich pineapple made here. Also included is a Maze ticket where you can enjoy trying to escape the famous pineapple themed maze.

Sea Life Park Hawaii Pass

Experience marine life up close and personal or from a distance with the Sea Life ticket. Enjoy the exhibits or sea lion and dolphin show provided where the trainers will show of their amazing tricks where you enjoy from a distance. Want to get up close and personal feed the turtles, tropical birds, and even swim with the dolphins at an extra additional cost.

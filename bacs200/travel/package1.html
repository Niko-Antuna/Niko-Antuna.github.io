Flight Ticket

This includes a roundtrip ticket to the destination of Oahu Hawaii for 10-days and back to the destination that you left from.

Car Rental

Transportation is offered where you have the choice of six rental car companies Avis, Budget, Enterprise, Hertz, National, and SIXT. Rental car companies will be located at the airport located on the ground level of Terminal 2 Parking Garage outside of baggage claim. An exception is Enterprise which is located on Aolele Street.

Note: Car rental can be deducted from charge if you are traveling with others who you will be sharing car rental with.

Hotel Booking

A booking at the hotel of the OHANA Waikiki East by Outrigger is in prime location. You will be off the beaten path while still being able to experience all Honolulu has to offer both during the day and during night life. Here you will be in walking distance of many activities such as Waikiki Beach, restaurants, vendors, shops, and malls such as the International Market Place.

Pearl Harbor National Memorial

At the Pearl Harbor National Memorial experience history first hand. Learn about one of the most pivotal moments in US history being the attack on Pearl Harbor, and the subsequent entry of the United States into World War II.

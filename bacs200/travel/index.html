Kualoa Adventure Package:

Experience Kualoa ranch like never before in our open-air 6 passenger ATV Raptor vehicles. Take turns driving through the Kualoa valley through off road trails deep into the scenic valley and to remote areas rarely visited by others. Our two-hour guided tour will take you through a WWII bunker used as an exhibit center with props and sets from movies/TV shows shot on the island. Also visit movie sites shot on our beautiful 1,000-acre Jurassic Valley.

Polynesian Cultural Center VIP Pass:

With the Super Ambassador Luau Package receive a private guided tour through 6 island villages throughout the day. Then during the evening enjoy the Ali’I Luau Buffet & Dinner Show. For the night portion experience the “Ha: Breath of Life” show with front two row seating.

Climbworks Keana Farms:

Our 3-hour guided zipline tour includes 8 world-class dual lines, 2 rappels, 3 sky bridges, ATV ride to the ridgetop, and Oahu’s longest zipline half a mile long. Here you will explore a working agricultural farm where you will learn about Hawaii’s rich history all while experiencing constant ocean and mountain views of the North Shore.

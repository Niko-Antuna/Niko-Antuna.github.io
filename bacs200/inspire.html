Bruce Lee

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore, who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

Bruce Lee is my hero and these are the characteristics I admire most about him.

  • Composed - His ability to have absolute confidence in himself being free of all fear and doubt
  • Wisdom - Had a great understanding about life and how to maneuver his way through life with the path of least resistance and success
  • Patience - Committed his whole life to expressing who he was as a human being
  • Pioneer - Was an innovator in he brought the origination of mix martial arts. Brought all the best fighting styles and techniques all together

These characteristics show why Bruce Lee was a hero to many. These attributes many people look to obtain in life and Bruce used these attributes to better his people and he led others by sharing his knowledge and wisdom with the world.
